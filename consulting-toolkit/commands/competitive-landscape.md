Build a competitive landscape analysis for: $ARGUMENTS

1. Identify the top 5-7 competitors in this space
2. For each competitor, gather:
   - Revenue / market share (if available)
   - Key differentiators
   - Strategic focus / recent moves
   - Strengths and weaknesses
3. Create a positioning map (describe axes and positions)
4. Identify white space and strategic implications
5. Output as structured markdown with comparison table
