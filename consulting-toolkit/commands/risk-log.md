description: Create or update a risk and issue log
---
Create/update a risk log for: $ARGUMENTS

For each risk/issue:
- ID
- Description
- Category (Strategic/Financial/Operational/Technical/External)
- Severity: Critical / Material / Minor
- Likelihood: High / Medium / Low
- Impact: High / Medium / Low
- Owner
- Mitigation plan
- Status (Open/Mitigated/Closed)
- Date identified

If a risk-log.md already exists, update it rather than replace it.
