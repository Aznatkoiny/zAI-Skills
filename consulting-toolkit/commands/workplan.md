---
description: Decompose an ambiguous ask into a structured workplan
---
Create a workplan for: $ARGUMENTS

1. Define the overall objective and success criteria
2. Break into 3-6 workstreams
3. For each workstream:
   - Key questions to answer
   - Analyses/activities required
   - Data/inputs needed
   - Owner (if applicable)
   - Dependencies on other workstreams
   - Estimated duration
4. Identify the critical path
5. Flag risks and assumptions
6. Present as a timeline view (week-by-week)
