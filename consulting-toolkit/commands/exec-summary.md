Write an executive summary for: $ARGUMENTS

Structure:
- SITUATION: The context and current state (2-3 sentences)
- COMPLICATION: What changed, what's at stake, why action is needed (2-3 sentences)
- RESOLUTION: The recommended path forward (2-3 sentences)
- KEY SUPPORTING POINTS: 3-5 bullets that back the resolution
- IMMEDIATE NEXT STEPS: 2-3 concrete actions with owners and timing

Keep the total length under 1 page. Write for a C-suite audience.
