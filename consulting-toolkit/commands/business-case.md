Build a business case for: $ARGUMENTS

Structure:
1. STRATEGIC RATIONALE: Why are we considering this?
2. OPTIONS ANALYSIS: 2-3 options with pros/cons
3. RECOMMENDED OPTION: Clear recommendation with reasoning
4. FINANCIAL IMPACT:
   - Investment required
   - Expected returns (NPV, ROI, payback period)
   - Key assumptions
5. IMPLEMENTATION PLAN: High-level phases and timeline
6. RISKS AND MITIGATIONS
7. DECISION REQUEST: What specifically needs to be approved

Create both a narrative document and a supporting financial model skeleton.
